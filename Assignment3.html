Museums and art galleries record historical changes and cultural accumulation and are worth visiting for literature and art lovers. In addition, modern museums combine the latest 3D display, animation simulation, and virtual reality technology based on collection and exhibition function so that the original dull show becomes interactive and the museum display is more exciting and intelligent. For young people busy with school and work, visiting museums and art galleries and seeing exhibitions in their free time is a way to experience various cultures and relax. The ability to learn while being entertained is what attracts young people.

More and more young people are willing to pay to visit museums and exhibitions that interest them, which brings more income to museums while also contributing to the development of local cultural tourism, so the market is objective.
