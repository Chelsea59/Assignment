Black Coffee

Black coffee is coffee without any modification. Black coffee brings the original feeling of tasting coffee. Black coffee combines the characteristics of coffee aroma, sweetness, alcohol, acid and bitterness.

Black coffee is mainly used in deep roasting, the process will add caramel, butter and other natural addition, and the coffee bean roasting to the degree of carbonization, so black coffee beans generally appear black, the deeper the roast degree of charcoal will be relatively heavier.

The sweetness of black coffee, and the bitterness of the surface is an integral relationship, so the fresh taste of good black coffee with sweet. Black COFFEE has ALWAYS BEEN known as "health messenger", and also enjoy the effect of "reducing weight", the caffeine of its connotation can accelerate fat decomposition.
