1. The Shepherd

According to a Roman linguist, Rostane Neroy (1613-1707) : Around age six centuries, an Arab shepherd he one day drive to Ethiopia grassland grazing sheep, see every goat appear happy, ecstatic, he felt very strange, after careful observation, found that these sheep are eating an red fruit to be excited, he tasted some curiously, find the fruit is very sweet and delicious, After eating, he also felt refreshed. From then on, he often drove the sheep to eat this delicious fruit with him. Then a Moslem passing by picked some of this incredible red fruit and took it home and gave it to other members of the congregation, so its magic effect spread.
