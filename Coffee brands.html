STARBUCKS in China

——We want to bring the Starbucks coffee heritage and unique coffee experience to our customers with every cup of coffee.

  • History

        The first Starbucks store in mainland China in 1999

        Starbucks opened its first store in mainland China in January 1999 at the China International Trade Center in Beijing. For Starbucks, China has become its fastest growing and largest overseas market.

  • Stores

        More than 4,100 stores are located in 168 cities in mainland China

        Currently, Starbucks has opened more than 4,100 stores in 168 cities in mainland China and has more than 57,000 Starbucks partners. This unique advantage enables us to deliver on our promise, every single day, in every Starbucks store.
