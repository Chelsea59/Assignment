Crystal Mountain Coffee in Cuba -Cubita

In Cuba, coffee cultivation is regulated by the state. The best coffee-growing areas in Cuba are in the central mountain range. Because in addition to growing coffee, this area also produces quartz, crystal and other precious minerals, so it is also called Crystal Mountain. Crystal Mountain is located adjacent to the Blue Mountains of Jamaica, with similar climate conditions and tastes similar to Blue Mountain coffee, which is comparable to the Blue Mountains of Jamaica. As a result, Cuba's Crystal Mountain has become the counterpart of Jamaica's Blue Mountain, which is also known as the "Blue Mountain of Cuba".
